Our Story

Founded in 1985, Road Side Grocery started as a small family farm stand on Route 9. What began as selling our own garden produce has grown into a beloved local institution, bringing the finest farm-fresh products to our community every single day.

We believe in sustainable farming, supporting local producers, and building relationships with our customers. Every item you see here has been personally selected for quality and freshness.
